History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform for innovation and collaboration.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower individuals and companies to innovate and solve real-world problems through technology.

Past Speakers

We've hosted pioneers like Ada Lovelace AI (2021), Linus Torvalds (2022), and Elon Musk (2023), all of whom made significant contributions to the tech world.
